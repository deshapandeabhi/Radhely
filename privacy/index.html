Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Welcome to Radhely. Your privacy is our absolute priority. We believe that your relationship's most intimate moments should stay private, secure, and protected.

1. AI Processing & Aria

Radhely uses advanced Artificial Intelligence (via OpenAI) to provide personalized relationship coaching and real-time insights (Aria). How we handle your data:

  • Chat data is processed strictly to provide you with coaching and suggestions.
  • Your private chats with Aria are never visible to your partner.
  • We do NOT sell your data or use your personal messages to train global AI models.

2. Encryption & Communication

All voice and video calling features in Radhely are powered by industry-standard encryption (via Agora RTC). This ensures that your calls are secure and cannot be intercepted or recorded by unauthorized parties.

3. Data Collection

We collect minimal information required to run the service, including your name, email, and basic relationship goals provided during the "Vibe Check" onboarding process.

4. Data Deletion Rights

You have full control over your data. You may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated relationship data at any time via our Account Deletion Page. Your data will be wiped immediately upon confirmation.

Alternatively, you may contact our support team at support@radhely.com if you encounter any issues.

5. Service Providers

We partner with secure infrastructure providers (Railway, Cloudinary, OpenAI, and Agora) to deliver a world-class experience. These providers are strictly used for technical processing and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
